# Product Page Wireframes — The Builders Academy

## Layout Principles

Based on Phase 1-2 research findings:

- **Above-the-fold priority:** Price, duration, "same-day certificate", trust badges, and CTA must be visible without scrolling
- **Single H1:** Course name only (audit found dual H1 tags causing SEO/hierarchy confusion)
- **4 Key Facts:** Duration, Certificate, Accreditation, Format — scannable at a glance
- **Trust signals next to CTA:** Accreditation badges (CPD, CITB) positioned near Add to Cart
- **Mobile sticky CTA:** Buy Now always one tap away (Strategic Pillar #2: Simplicity)
- **Cross-sell:** Related courses section for upsell (conversion journey friction point)
- **Reviews visible:** Trustpilot rating near price (Strategic Pillar #1: Trust First)

- Animation: slideUp 300ms ease-out

## Section-by-Section Layout Rationale

### Breadcrumbs
- Shortened on mobile (Home > ... > CSCS Green)
- Full path on desktop for SEO and navigation
- Links to parent categories for discovery

### Product Hero (Above the Fold)
- **Left:** Product image/course preview thumbnail
- **Right:** Title (H1), subtitle, price box with CTA
- **Price box:** Price, star rating, review count, Add to Cart, Buy Now, trust badges
- **Why:** All critical decision factors above fold (conversion journey finding #1)
- **Mobile:** Image → title → price → badges (linear flow)

### Key Facts
- **4 fact cards:** Duration, Certificate, Accreditation, Format
- **Why:** Strategic Pillar #2 (Simplicity) — lead with 4 key facts
- **Icons:** Visual scanning aid for Dave persona (quick decision-maker)

### What You'll Learn + Who Is This For
- **Desktop:** Side-by-side columns
- **Mobile:** Stacked sections
- **Why:** Scannable bullets, not dense paragraphs; addresses "is this right for me?" question

### How It Works
- **4 steps:** Purchase → Complete → Pass → Download
- **Why:** Terry persona needs process reassurance; reduces uncertainty
- **"Unlimited retakes":** Reduces anxiety about failure

### Reviews
- **Trustpilot integration:** Star rating, review count, sample reviews
- **Why:** Strategic Pillar #1 (Trust First); reviews near decision point
- **Mobile:** Swipeable carousel

### FAQ
- **Collapsible accordion:** 4-5 common questions
- **Why:** Reduces support queries; addresses common objections before checkout
- **SEO:** Structured data opportunity for FAQ schema

### Complete Your Training (Cross-sell)
- **3 related course cards**
- **Bulk quote CTA for employers**
- **Why:** Increases AOV (target +15%); addresses employer discovery path

### Mobile Sticky CTA
- **Fixed bottom bar:** Price + Buy Now
- **Why:** ~70% mobile traffic; CTA always accessible without scrolling back up
- **Trust micro-copy:** "Secure checkout" + "30-day refund" reduces purchase anxiety
