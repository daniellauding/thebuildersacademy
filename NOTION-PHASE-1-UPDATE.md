## 🛤️ Conversion Journeys (2)

### Journey 1: Direct Search → Product (70% of traffic)

**Flow:**
```
Google → Product Page → Add to Cart → Checkout → Purchase
```

**11 Friction Points Identified:**
1. **Above-the-fold trust** (Critical) — No reviews, badges, or trust signals visible without scrolling
2. **Mobile tap targets** (Critical) — CTA buttons too small on mobile
3. **Price clarity** (High) — Price not immediately obvious
4. **Accreditation clarity** (High) — CPD/CITB badges buried in description
5. **Product description length** (Moderate) — Too much text, poor scannability
6. **Related courses** (Moderate) — No upsell or "you might also need" suggestions
7. **Guest checkout missing** (Critical) — Forces account creation
8. **Payment options limited** (High) — No Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
9. **Checkout steps** (Moderate) — 3+ steps feels long on mobile
10. **Trust during payment** (High) — No security badges near card input
11. **Post-purchase clarity** (Low) — Unclear when certificate arrives

**Quick Wins (High Impact, Low Effort):**
- Add Trustpilot widget above the fold
- Enlarge mobile CTA buttons (48px+ tap targets)
- Add security badges at checkout
- Enable guest checkout

---

### Journey 2: Discovery → Category → Product (30% of traffic)

**Flow:**
```
Google → Homepage → Category → Product → Checkout → Purchase
```

**12 Friction Points Identified:**
1. **Homepage clarity** (Critical) — No clear entry points by user need
2. **Navigation overload** (Critical) — 109+ links → cognitive paralysis
3. **Category page layout** (High) — Course cards hard to scan, no filters
4. **Search missing** (High) — No prominent search bar
5. **Mobile navigation** (Critical) — Hamburger menu is overwhelming
6. **"For Employers" missing** (High) — No bulk purchase pathway
7. **Category descriptions** (Moderate) — Missing category-level SEO content
8. **Filters missing** (High) — Can't filter by price, duration, or accreditation
9. **Sort options missing** (Moderate) — Can't sort by popularity or price
10. **Breadcrumbs missing** (Low) — Hard to navigate back
11. **Course comparison** (Moderate) — Can't compare courses side-by-side
12. **Exit intent** (Moderate) — No pop-up or retention strategy

**Quick Wins:**
- Add prominent search with autocomplete
- Reduce navigation to 5-7 top categories
- Add "For Employers" link in header
- Add category filters (price, duration, accreditation)

## 🧭 Strategic Direction — 5 Pillars

### 1. Trust First
**Why:** 20-60+ builders need to trust site before entering payment details on unfamiliar site.

**How:**
- Trustpilot reviews + star rating above the fold
- CPD/CITB badges next to "Add to Cart"
- "Trusted by" employer logos (Skanska, Kier, G4S, BBC Studios)
- Professional, clean design
- Visible phone number + live chat

---

### 2. Simplicity Over Features
**Why:** Cognitive overload from 109+ nav links, long course names, cluttered layouts.

**How:**
- Reduce navigation to 5-7 clear categories
- Product pages: 4 key facts (Price, Duration, Certificate, Accreditation)
- Mobile: sticky CTA bar
- Checkout: minimize steps, enable guest checkout
- Homepage: clear entry points ("I need a CSCS card" / "I need safety training")

---

### 3. Mobile-First Reality
**Why:** Significant mobile traffic — tradespeople checking courses on breaks, on the van.

**How:**
- Design mobile first, enhance for desktop
- Large tap targets (48px min, 56px preferred)
- Readable text (16px+ body)
- Streamlined mobile checkout
- Apple Pay / Google Pay integration
- Test on actual mobile devices

---

### 4. Employer Revenue Growth
**Why:** Employer/bulk purchases = 10-50x AOV vs individual buyer.

**How:**
- "For Employers" / "For Business" pathway from homepage
- Bulk pricing or "Request a Quote" for 5+ licences
- Training matrix by role
- Invoice/PO support at checkout
- Employer dashboard for tracking completions
- Employer case studies and testimonials

---

### 5. Smart Discovery
**Why:** Users who don't land on product page need help finding right course.

**How:**
- Search with autocomplete + informal terms ("green card" → CSCS Green Card)
- Category filters: price, duration, accreditation, job role
- "Most Popular" and "Best Sellers" on category pages
- Related courses on product pages
- Career pathway view: "You have X → Next get Y"

## 🚨 Critical Risks

1. **Magento platform constraints** — Must work within Magento/Adobe Commerce
2. **Low digital literacy audience** — Over-design = lost conversions
3. **Mobile-first reality** — Desktop-first design = revenue drop
4. **Trust erosion during transition** — Visual change could temporarily reduce trust
5. **Navigation overload** — Simplifying could confuse returning customers
6. **SEO ranking disruption** — URL/structure changes need careful migration

---

## 🎯 Key Strategic Insight

> **"Make it obvious, make it trusted, make it easy — especially on mobile, especially for people who aren't comfortable online."**

In a market where every competitor's website looks outdated and transactional, a modernized, trust-forward, accessibility-first redesign would be a **genuine competitive moat** — not just a cosmetic improvement.
