# Employer Pathway Wireframes — The Builders Academy

## Layout Principles

Based on Phase 1-2 research findings:

- **Employer Revenue Growth:** Strategic Pillar #4 — target 10% of total revenue from employers
- **Sarah persona:** Desktop-first, wants team management, bulk pricing, invoice support
- **Clear value proposition:** Bulk training, progress tracking, compliance records
- **Dual CTAs:** "Request a Quote" (primary) + Phone number (secondary)
- **Training matrix:** Show courses by job role (key employer need)
- **Social proof:** Employer testimonials + logos (different from individual reviews)
- **Dashboard preview:** Concept of team management capabilities

## Section-by-Section Layout Rationale

### Hero
- **Headline:** "Train Your Construction Team" — speaks to Sarah persona's goal
- **Value props as checkmarks:** Dashboard, bulk pricing, invoice, records, accreditations
- **Dual CTA:** Quote form (primary) + phone number (secondary)
- **Why:** Sarah needs to justify ROI; checkmarks address her decision criteria

### How It Works
- **3 steps:** Request → Setup → Training
- **Why:** Simple process overview reduces friction for first-time employer buyers

### Training Packages
- **Pre-built bundles:** Site Worker Essentials, Supervisor, Custom
- **Savings highlighted:** "Save 25% vs individual purchase"
- **Why:** Bundles increase AOV and simplify the employer decision (Strategic Pillar #4)
- **"Most Popular" badge:** Social proof for decision paralysis

### Training by Role
- **4 role cards:** Labourer, Supervisor, Manager, All Site Staff
- **Course count + starting price per card**
- **Why:** Sarah persona thinks in terms of roles, not individual courses

### Dashboard Preview
- **Mock dashboard UI:** Staff count, completion progress, recent activity, expiring certs
- **Why:** Sarah needs to see team management capability before committing
- **Expiry warning:** Shows proactive renewal support (retention feature)

### Employer Testimonials
- **Different from individual reviews:** Focus on scale, savings, compliance
- **Named companies with logos:** Skanska, Kier — credible construction brands
- **Why:** B2B decisions require peer validation from similar companies

### Quote Request Form
- **Inline on page:** No separate contact page (reduces clicks)
- **6 fields:** Company, Name, Email, Phone, Staff count, Training needed
- **Staff count dropdown:** Pre-qualifies lead size
- **Why:** Embedded form converts better than "Contact Us" links
- **Phone fallback:** "Prefer to talk?" for high-value leads who want human contact
