# The Builders Academy - Redesign Project

**Client:** The Builders Academy  
**URL:** https://thebuildersacademy.co.uk  
**Platform:** Magento/Adobe Commerce  
**Industry:** Construction Training & E-Learning  

---

## 🎯 Project Goal

Modernize an established e-learning e-commerce website in the construction training industry. Improve:
- Visual design & brand modernization
- Usability & trust signals
- Course discovery & categorization
- Conversion rate optimization
- Mobile experience
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA)

---

## 👥 Target Audience

- **Builders** (tradespeople, 20-60+)
- **Employers** (construction companies buying packages)
- **Returning buyers**
- **Mixed digital literacy** (older users, mobile-first users)

---

## 🔑 Primary Goals

1. Increase trust & credibility
2. Improve course discovery
3. Increase package sales
4. Modernize brand presence
5. Better mobile experience

---

## 📊 Key Pages to Redesign

1. **Home page** — First impression, course discovery
2. **Product pages** — Course details, CTAs, trust signals
3. **Category pages** — Course browsing, filtering
4. **Package pages** — Bundle offerings for employers

### Phases
1. **Business Goals & Research** — Define purpose, personas, conversion funnels
2. **Full Audit** — UX, heuristics, content, IA, SEO, accessibility, competitive
3. **Information Architecture** — Sitemap, navigation, categorization
4. **UX Structure** — Wireframes, flows, CTA strategy
5. **UI Modernization** — Visual direction, typography, color, components
6. **Design System** — Scalable component library for Figma
7. **Accessibility** — WCAG 2.2 AA compliance
8. **SEO & Content** — Structured data, heading hierarchy
9. **Testing Plan** — Usability tests, A/B tests, metrics
10. **Execution** — Team model with risks, recommendations, warnings
